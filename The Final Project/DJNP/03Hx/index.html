Robot Has Got The Blues

The seminal work of DJ Nickelpawn, first played at the concert commemorating Robot Rock and the end of the war on the Shoebox Rebellion. The album tells the story of a twenty-four hour day in the life of a Machinae, surviving a life in the Inner City.

Symphonium Starless

The album dedicated to the heros and heartaches of the Starless Knights. Long remembered as the anthem of the Pow Riots. The album was a gift from Nickelpawn to the first Starless to be nominated for a City Council seat in the Acid Quadrant.

Mishui-Songs

A new ambient collection dedicated to the mysterious yokai of the Stacks. Written in collaboration with DJ Jazzy Jade.
