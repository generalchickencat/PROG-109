ABOUT THE DJ

one machine * one soul * one rabbit * many holes

DJ Nicklepawn was uncovered by the Shoebox Rebellion over over one hundred and seventy years ago. His hybernation had been long enough to wipe all memory of any life before the boxing. Though the original chasis given to him was a series of temporary fragments slowly modified and upgraded by friends at the Broken Cogg, Nicklepawn eventually found favor with an elder Queen of the Zanthenum, and was gifted with the rare artifact of an old performance art chasis from before the Chaos Wars. This unique body was a combination of zaithenoid and lepus, a purely machinae White Rabbit.

Today DJ Nicklepawn is a resident of the Inner City once again, haunting the likes of New Arcadia, the Borderlands of Clover, and various underground shows in Freighton.
